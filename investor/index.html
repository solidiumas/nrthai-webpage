Demand is enormous, but the market has a gap: almost no one delivers secure solutions with the option to run locally, and very few deliver finished turnkey solutions for a niche. Most have to buy an AI subscription and make everything work themselves. We deliver everything out of the box with short onboarding.

RundenThe round

Vi henter kapital via SLIP. We're raising via a SLIP.

En SLIP (Startup's Lead Investment Paper) er det norske motstykket til en SAFE, utviklet for tidligfase. Du investerer i dag, og aksjene tegnes ved neste emisjon — med rabatt og et avtalt verditak. Det gir oss fart uten å låse en for tidlig verdivurdering, og gir deg forutsigbarhet. A SLIP (Startup's Lead Investment Paper) is the Norwegian counterpart to a SAFE, built for early stage. You invest today, and shares are issued at the next funding round — with a discount and an agreed valuation cap. It gives us speed without locking in a premature valuation, and gives you predictability.
