Company · Bergen · est. 2026

Infrastructure for enterprise AI agents.

Nrth AI is a pre-seed startup based in Bergen, Norway. We're building the next-generation operating system for enterprise AI agents — secure, accountable, and running on your own hardware.

✦ 01 · Mission

Everyone wants AI. Almost no one can run it safely.

Demand for AI is enormous, but most companies are handed a subscription and left to wire everything together themselves — and sending sensitive data to servers they don't control isn't an option. We're building the layer that closes that gap: a secure runtime that turns models, agents, and real workflows into one accountable system you actually own.

✦ 02 · Why now

The timing isn't an accident.

Four forces make this the moment to build the OS — not in two years.

01Sovereignty

Data sovereignty isn't optional

For regulated industries, manufacturing, energy, and maritime, shipping data to servers abroad is a non-starter. On-premise stops being a nice-to-have and becomes the requirement.

On-prem or nothing
02Economics

The "AI employee" math

A skilled employee in Norway costs 1–1.5 MNOK a year. An equivalent AI system costs 15–35% of that, works around the clock, and never churns.

15–35% of a hire
03Gap

Most teams get tools, not systems

The market hands people parts and a subscription. Very few deliver the finished, accountable system that actually does the work — which is exactly what the OS is.

Finished, not parts
04Reach

Borderless from day one

The OS deploys anywhere — cloud or a customer's own hardware. The need is real and global, so we're built to ship across borders from the start.

Deploy anywhere
